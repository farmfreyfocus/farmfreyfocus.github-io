About Us

Farm_Frey Focus is a platform that brings together farmers,breeders and all agricultural stakeholders to conduct a range of agricultural and pastoral activities in technological context

Features

HOW THE COMPANY DOES WORK:

Marketing platform

It's a place where farmer,breeders and all agricultural stakeholders meet to agree on crop prices as well as agricultural inputs in a state of freedom and transparency.

Market forecasting

By using the artificial intelligence engine,farmers will be able to get accurate pricing information on various markets nationalwise as well as internationally hence,alse with high demand for certain crops using intuitive algorithms so it helpful to small farmers.This will facilitate higher production of agricultural products to feed the world as the market is no longer an obstacle

Weather forecasting

In conjuction with weather agencies(for example Tanzania weather agencies) its a place where rainfall information will be shown(for instance rainfall history) at various region in africa.

Budget

is a section that gives farmer awareness upon the estimations and forecasting of the agricultural or herding plan in a accordance to the size of his or her area.

Store

Is a place where are farmer will be able to keep his important agricultural information from one season to another which will give him an assessment of the growth/decline of his production process.

Library

A section where the farmer will be able to increase his/her knowledge through various articles,researches,journals and books from various sources.
